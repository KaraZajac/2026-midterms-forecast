# 2026 Senate Race Ratings — May 2026

**As of: 2026-05-25**

Three major handicappers — Cook Political Report, Sabato's Crystal Ball, and Inside Elections — converge on a narrow set of competitive races. The genuine toss-ups are **Maine, Michigan, and Ohio (Special)**, with **Georgia** and **North Carolina** sitting in Lean D / Toss-up territory. Outside that tier, **New Hampshire** leans Democrat (Pappas vs. Sununu); **Iowa, Texas, Montana, Alaska, Nebraska, Florida-Sp** sit at Likely R; **Minnesota** sits at Likely D. Of the 35 seats on the ballot, only ~9 are genuinely in play, and only 4-5 are realistic flip opportunities for Democrats.

Cook moved **Ohio-Sp from Lean R to Toss-up** in spring 2026 as Brown's polling edged toward parity with appointed Sen. Husted, and Sabato moved **Montana from Safe R to Likely R** on March 4, 2026 in light of an unsettled GOP open primary. Inside Elections is the only outlet still rating Maine as Tilt R rather than Toss-up, despite Platner's leads in May polling — a possible lag indicator.
